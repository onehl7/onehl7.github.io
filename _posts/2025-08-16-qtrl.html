Quantum Technology Readiness Levels (QTRL)

LevelDescription
QTRL 1Theoretical framework for quantum computing is formulated.
QTRL 2Basic device principles studied; algorithms formulated.
QTRL 3Fabricated physical qubits exist; lab studies validate theory.
QTRL 4Multi-qubit systems fabricated; classical control devices developed.
QTRL 5Components integrated into small processor without error correction.
QTRL 6Components integrated into small processor with error correction.
QTRL 7Prototype quantum computer solves small but user-relevant problems.
QTRL 8Scalable version of quantum computer completed and qualified.
QTRL 9Quantum computers exceed classical power for specific problems.
